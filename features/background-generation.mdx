## From playroom to fantasy world

One of Cinemachine's most magical features is transforming the simple playroom background into rich, imaginative worlds. Whether your child's story takes place in an enchanted forest, a medieval castle, or outer space, the system generates custom backgrounds that match the narrative.

<Card title="One-shot generation" icon="sparkles">
  Each scene gets a single, carefully crafted background image. This approach ensures visual consistency throughout the scene while avoiding temporal artifacts that can occur with frame-by-frame generation.
</Card>

## How it works

### Story context from voice interaction

Before generating backgrounds, Cinemachine's voice agent has a conversation with your child about their story. Through natural dialogue, the system learns:

- What kind of world the story takes place in
- The mood and atmosphere (dark forest, sunny meadow, etc.)
- Specific details your child wants to include
- The overall narrative context

<Info>
  The voice agent uses the Gemini Live API to understand your child's creative vision and translate it into detailed visual descriptions for background generation.
</Info>

### Generating with Nanobanana Pro

Cinemachine uses **Nanobanana Pro**, an advanced image generation model, to create the fantasy backgrounds. Based on the story context gathered from the voice conversation, the system:

1. Constructs a detailed prompt describing the desired world
2. Generates a high-quality background image
3. Creates one background per scene (static throughout that scene)

<Accordion title="What is Nanobanana Pro?">
  Nanobanana Pro is a powerful image generation model optimized for creating detailed, coherent scenes. In Cinemachine, it's used not only for backgrounds but also for generating character features like eyes and mouths.
</Accordion>

### Static backgrounds for consistency

Unlike approaches that regenerate the background for every frame, Cinemachine uses a **single background image per scene**. This design choice offers several benefits:

- **No temporal consistency issues** — The background never flickers or shifts unexpectedly
- **Faster processing** — Only one generation per scene instead of hundreds
- **Predictable quality** — Each background is carefully crafted and can be reviewed
- **Lower computational cost** — Makes the service more accessible and sustainable

## Scene composition

Once the background is generated, the segmented toy cutouts are composited onto it:

```
Generated Fantasy Background
        +
Tracked Toy Cutout (frame-by-frame)
        =
Toy moving through a fantasy world
```

The toy's position data from the segmentation phase ensures it moves naturally through the new environment, maintaining the original motion your child created during play.

<Note>
  Because the background is static and only the toy moves, the system never needs to solve the complex problem of hand inpainting or background restoration.
</Note>

## Quality and style

Nanobanana Pro generates backgrounds that:

- Match the aesthetic described in the story
- Use appropriate color palettes for the mood
- Include relevant environmental details (trees, buildings, stars, etc.)
- Maintain visual clarity so the toy remains the focus

<Warning>
  **Scene transitions**: When your child's story moves from one location to another, the voice agent identifies this as a new scene. Each scene gets its own unique background, creating distinct visual chapters in the final movie.
</Warning>

## Technical considerations

### One background per scene

The system determines scene boundaries based on:

- Explicit scene changes mentioned in the story
- Changes in location or setting
- Natural breaks in the narrative flow

Each scene maintains its background throughout, even if the toy moves around within that scene.

### Resolution and quality

Backgrounds are generated at resolutions that match or exceed the original footage quality, ensuring the final composite looks crisp and professional.

## What happens next

After background generation and compositing:

1. **Eyes and mouth overlays** are added to the toy
2. **Lip sync** matches mouth movements to dialogue
3. **Mild body animation** brings subtle life to the character

The result is a fully realized animated movie where toys become characters in the worlds your child imagined.
