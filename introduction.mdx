## What is Cinemachine?

Cinemachine is a creative storytelling platform that transforms the way kids play with toys. With just a fixed camera setup and their imagination, children can record themselves playing with their favorite toys and watch as Cinemachine transforms raw footage into professional-looking animated movies.

The magic happens behind the scenes: Cinemachine removes hands from the video, generates fantasy backgrounds based on the story, adds expressive eyes and mouth animations to toys, and syncs lip movements to dialogue. What starts as a simple recording session becomes an epic adventure in a castle, forest, or any world your child imagines.

## Who is Cinemachine for?

Cinemachine is designed for:

- **Kids** who love playing with toys and want to create their own animated movies
- **Parents** looking to encourage creative storytelling and digital literacy
- **Educators** seeking engaging tools for narrative development and media literacy
- **Creative families** who want to capture and enhance playtime memories

## What makes Cinemachine special?

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Fixed camera simplicity">
    Unlike complex motion capture setups, Cinemachine uses a simple fixed camera. Just set it up once, and kids can focus entirely on their creative storytelling without technical distractions.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="AI-powered video processing">
    Advanced AI separates toys from hands and backgrounds, generates fantasy worlds, and adds character animations. The technology is sophisticated, but the experience is simple and magical.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Voice-to-voice interaction">
    Kids describe their story naturally through conversation with an AI voice agent. No typing, no complex interfaces—just talk about the adventure you want to create.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Expressive character animation">
    Toys come alive with generated eyes, lip-synced mouth movements, and subtle body animations that make them feel like real characters in a movie.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Example workflow

Here's how a typical Cinemachine session works:

1. **Set up** your fixed camera pointing at your play area
2. **Record** yourself playing with toys, speaking their dialogue
3. **Describe** your story to the voice agent ("They're in a magical forest")
4. **Watch** as Cinemachine transforms your recording into an animated movie

The result? A polished animated movie where toys perform against fantasy backgrounds, with expressive faces and synchronized lip movements—all created from simple toy play.
